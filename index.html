Who We Are

Built By Workers,
For Workers.

VANGUARD was born from frustration — professionals across every industry, tired of empty promises and slow change. We are not a lobby. We are not a corporation. We are a movement forged by the people who clock in every day.

From tech to healthcare, education to logistics — we organize, advocate, and win. Our network spans 60+ cities and every action we take is driven by one question: does this actually help our members?

Who can join VANGUARD?
VANGUARD is open to any employed or self-employed professional, regardless of sector, job title, or country of residence. Whether you're a full-time employee, freelancer, contractor, or gig worker — if you work, you belong here. Membership starts at $8/month.
What legal support is included in my membership?
All members receive access to our legal helpline (Mon–Sat, 8am–10pm), document review services, template contracts and letters, and a first free consultation with a VANGUARD-partnered labor attorney. Members facing active disputes may qualify for full representation — assessed case by case.
How does the anonymous reporting portal work?
Our portal uses end-to-end encryption and assigns a randomized case ID — your identity is never tied to your report unless you choose to reveal it. Our compliance team reviews submissions and, if action is warranted, proceeds only with your explicit consent. Read our privacy policy →
Is there a strike fund, and how do I access it?
Yes. VANGUARD maintains a collective action fund of over $1.2M, pooled from member contributions. Members who have been active for at least 90 days and are participating in an approved collective action may apply for financial support. Applications are reviewed within 48 hours.
How is VANGUARD different from a traditional union?
Traditional unions are typically tied to a single employer or industry. VANGUARD is a cross-sector, worker-owned network — meaning our power and resources aren't siloed. Your membership moves with you across jobs, cities, and careers. We also move faster: our digital-first infrastructure cuts bureaucracy and gets you help in hours, not months.
Can I cancel my membership at any time?
Yes, with zero penalties. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. Your access continues through the end of your billing cycle. If you cancel while an active case is in progress, our legal team will transition your case responsibly and not abandon any ongoing proceedings.
How does the Salary Transparency Index protect privacy?
All salary submissions are anonymized immediately upon entry — we collect role, industry, region, and experience level, but never names, employer names, or identifying details. Data is aggregated and only displayed when a minimum threshold of submissions exist, preventing any individual from being identified.
Does VANGUARD operate internationally?
We currently have active chapters in 22 countries across North America, Europe, Latin America, and Southeast Asia. Members in regions without a local chapter are supported through our digital-first programs. We are actively expanding — if you want to launch a chapter in your city, apply here →
